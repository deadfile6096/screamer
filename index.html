Look closer... I can see you.

You open your eyes.

It smells of dust and decay.

Moonlight breaks through broken boards.

In the corner — a door. On the floor — a shattered mirror.

You enter the corridor.

The floor creaks beneath your feet.

A dim lamp flickers on the wall.

To the left — stairs going up. To the right — a room with an open door.

The room is empty.

A radio stands in the corner.

It turns on by itself and whispers:

"He's still here..."

You find a secret door in the wall.

It's covered with strange symbols.

Nearby — a broken window.

You feel someone watching you...

Today you were smarter than the darkness.

But it's still watching...
